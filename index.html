AI should not stop at giving you text.

When people ask AI to plan, organize, route, schedule, or shop, the answer should be portable. AIX is a proposed exchange layer that describes the user's intent in a form other software can understand.

1. Ask AI“Plan my fishing trip,” “make a playlist,” or “build a grocery list.”
→
2. Create AIXThe request becomes a structured AI Intent Exchange file.
→
3. Export ActionsAdapters convert the intent into files apps already accept.

One intent. Many applications.

AIX starts as a simple file format and grows into a translation layer between AI and execution platforms.

🗺️

Travel routes

Convert trip plans into Google Maps links, GPX, ICS, and CSV files for maps, GPS devices, and calendars.

🎵

Playlists

Turn a theme, mood, or event into a portable playlist intent that music services can interpret.

🛒

Shopping lists

Convert recipes, budgets, diets, or meal plans into structured grocery lists and future retail integrations.
